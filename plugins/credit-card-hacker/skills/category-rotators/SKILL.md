# category-rotators — the 5% earner cards

## When to load

- User mentions Discover IT, Freedom Flex, Customized Cash, or Custom Cash
- Quarterly portfolio review (new quarter activated)
- Mapping organic spend to maximize earning rates
- Recommending a no-AF "supporting" card alongside a transferable-points card

## The four major rotators

### Chase Freedom Flex (5% rotating quarters)
- 5x UR on rotating categories, capped at $1,500/quarter ($75 max bonus per quarter)
- Categories announced each quarter; activate online before the quarter starts
- Common categories: gas + EV charging, grocery, drugstores, Amazon, PayPal, Walmart, streaming
- Pairs with CSP/CSR/Ink to convert UR to transferable points (otherwise it's cashback)

### Discover IT (5% rotating quarters)
- 5% cashback on rotating categories, capped at $1,500/quarter
- First-year cashback match doubles all cashback earned (effectively 10% on rotating cats Y1)
- Discover business reports to personal credit (counts toward 5/24)

### BoA Customized Cash Rewards (3% choose-your-category)
- 3% cashback on one chosen category (gas, online shopping, dining, travel, drug stores, home improvement) up to $2,500/qtr combined with 2% grocery
- Multiplied by Preferred Rewards tier (Platinum Honors = 1.75× → 5.25% on chosen category)
- BoA-asset-gather sensitive — value depends on having $20K+ at BoA/Merrill

### Citi Custom Cash (5% top spend category up to $500/mo)
- Auto-rewards 5% on whichever Citi-defined category you spend the most on each statement, capped at $500/month
- 17 eligible categories. Set-and-forget, no activation needed.
- Pairs with Strata Premier to make TYP transferable

## Strategy patterns

### "Quintuple stack" pattern (P1+P2 each holding all four)
Couple holds 4 rotators each = 8 cards rotating quarterly. Coverage approaches 100% of common categories at 5%. Overhead: tracking quarter activations.

### Single-rotator pattern
For solo users not maxing the stack, pick the rotator that aligns with your top organic category:
- Heavy gas commuter → Custom Cash (gas at 5%/$500/mo) OR Freedom Flex when gas is in rotation
- Heavy grocery → Amex Gold (4x MR uncapped) wins, but no-AF backup is BoA Customized 3% grocery
- Heavy dining → Custom Cash dining 5%/$500 OR Amex Gold

### Q4 rotation patterns to watch
- Freedom Flex Q4 historically includes PayPal — chain via Visa GCs at PayPal-eligible merchants
- Discover IT Q4 historically includes Amazon — pre-buy gift cards in Q4 for Q1 spending

## When NOT to recommend a rotator

- User has 5/24 burn from prior cards and doesn't have room for another inquiry
- Discover business specifically — counts toward 5/24, gives small SUB, often not worth the slot
- User won't track quarterly activations (Freedom Flex / Discover IT require manual activation; Custom Cash and Customized Cash are auto)
